About LOC

ACM seeks to impose as few rules as possible in order to encourage maximum collaboration and innovation, but there are a few important things to make note of to ensure betterment. 1. Teams can be from one to four members. 2. The reporting time is sharp 8:30am on the 16th of March. 3. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner will be provided. 4. All participants are requested to get their respective Identity Cards. 5. All coders are requested to get their laptops. The solution must be developed during the event only. 6. Teams will be shortlisted on the basis of their resume. 7. Registrations close on the 12th of March at 5pm. 8. Selected teams will receive a message from ACM by 14th of March. Only those teams are requested to be present at the venue on the 16th of March at 8:30 am. One of the most awaited events of DJ-ACM,LoC(Lines of Code) is around the corner. With over 200 participants from numerous colleges, the hackathon is a gathering where individual programmers collaborate their codes to make industry-grade projects .

Testing both, coding and coordination skills, LoC is split into 2 rounds. So, gear up and use this platform to showcase your excellence in various programming domains.
